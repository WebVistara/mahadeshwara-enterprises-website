Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers about our services, areas, and process

What rock cutting services do you provide?

We offer diamond blade cutting, chemical expansive splitting, controlled blasting, and wire saw cutting for all rock types. Our methods ensure precision and safety for residential, commercial, and infrastructure projects.

Do you provide excavation machines on rent?

Yes, we offer JCB mini excavators, large hydraulic excavators, tractor compressors, and power zensets on daily, weekly, or monthly rental basis. All machines are well‑maintained and operated by skilled drivers if needed.

Which areas do you serve?

We primarily serve Bangalore and surrounding areas up to 100 km, including Whitefield, Electronic City, Yeshwanthpur, Hebbal, and Tumkur Road. For larger projects, we may extend further – please contact us.

How can I request a quotation?

You can call us directly at 98450 89210 or fill the inquiry form on our website. We typically respond within 2 hours and offer a free site visit to assess the project.

Do you provide demolition services?

Absolutely. We handle both partial and complete demolition of structures – from small buildings to industrial sheds. Our team uses hydraulic breakers, diamond saws, and follows safety protocols to ensure debris removal and site clearance.

Do you also do concrete cutting and core drilling?

Yes, we specialise in concrete cutting (wall sawing, slab sawing) and core drilling for openings, utility lines, and HVAC installations. We use dust‑controlled equipment for indoor work.

Still have questions?

Our team is ready to help. Call us or drop a message – we usually reply within an hour.
